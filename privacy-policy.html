Privacy Policy

Effective Date: February 5, 2026

Zebra Code Scanner ("the App") is developed and operated by Viral Apps Studio ("we", "us", or "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App collects, how it is used, and the choices you have.

Summary: Zebra Code Scanner is designed with privacy in mind. The App does not require account creation, does not collect personal information, and stores all your data locally on your device. Network requests are only made when you scan a barcode to look up product information.

1. Information We Collect

Information Stored Locally on Your Device

All data the App creates is stored exclusively on your device using Apple's Core Data framework. This includes:

  • Generated Codes: QR codes and barcodes you create, including the code type, content, and generated image.
  • Scanned Codes: Codes you scan, including the decoded content and any product information retrieved.
  • App Preferences: Your settings such as vibration feedback, sound feedback, and appearance mode (light/dark/system).

This data never leaves your device unless you explicitly choose to share it using the iOS share sheet.

Information We Do Not Collect

The App does not collect:

  • Personal identification information (name, email, phone number, address)
  • Account credentials (no sign-up or login required)
  • Location data
  • Device advertising identifiers (IDFA)
  • Browsing or usage analytics
  • Contact information (contacts are accessed only locally for vCard QR code generation and are never transmitted)

2. Device Permissions

The App requests the following permissions, each only when the relevant feature is used:

Permission Purpose
Camera Required to scan QR codes and barcodes using the device camera.
Photo Library (Read) Allows importing images from your photo library for scanning.
Photo Library (Write) Allows saving generated QR codes and barcodes to your photo library.
Contacts Allows selecting a contact to generate a vCard QR code. Contact data is processed locally and never transmitted.

You can revoke any of these permissions at any time through your device's Settings app.

3. Third-Party Services

The App communicates with third-party services only in the following limited circumstances:

Product Information Lookup

When you scan a barcode, the App may send the barcode number (and only the barcode number) to the following services to retrieve product information:

  • Open Food Facts — A free, open-source food product database. Used to look up food product details.
  • UPCitemdb — A product information database. Used as a fallback for non-food products.

No personal information, device identifiers, or location data is sent with these requests. Only the scanned barcode number is transmitted.

Crash Reporting

The App uses Firebase Crashlytics by Google to collect crash reports. When the App crashes, a report is automatically sent that may include:

  • Device type and operating system version
  • App version and build number
  • Crash logs and stack traces

Crash reports do not contain personal information, scan history, or generated code content. They are used solely to identify and fix bugs to improve app stability.

Remote Configuration

The App uses Firebase Remote Config to check the minimum required app version. This is used only to notify you if a critical update is available. No personal data is transmitted in this process.

Analytics and Advertising

The App does not use analytics tracking. The App does not display advertisements and does not use advertising SDKs.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties.

Data is shared with third parties only as described in Section 3 above (barcode numbers for product lookup, and crash reports for stability improvement).

You may choose to share generated QR codes or barcodes using the iOS share sheet. This sharing is entirely user-initiated and controlled by you.

5. Data Retention

Data Type Storage Retention
Generated codes On your device Until you delete them or uninstall the App
Scanned codes On your device Until you delete them or uninstall the App
App preferences On your device Until you uninstall the App
Crash reports Firebase servers 90 days (Google's default retention)

6. Your Choices and Controls

You have full control over your data:

  • Delete History: Use Settings → Clear History to permanently delete all generated and scanned code records from your device.
  • Revoke Permissions: You can revoke camera, photo library, or contacts permissions at any time in your device's Settings.
  • Delete the App: Uninstalling the App removes all locally stored data from your device.

7. Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data stored on your device is protected by iOS's built-in file encryption.
  • All network communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption.
  • The App does not store sensitive credentials or authentication tokens.

8. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. Since the App does not collect personal information from any user, no special provisions for children's data are necessary.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Effective Date." Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
